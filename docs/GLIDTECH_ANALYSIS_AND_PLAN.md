# Glidtech Design Analysis & Implementation Plan

## 1. Analysis of Glīd Technology (Inspiration Source)

**URL:** [https://www.glidtech.us/](https://www.glidtech.us/)
**Core Concept:** Autonomous Dual-Mode Logistics (Road/Rail).
**Design Aesthetic:** Industrial, futuristic, high-precision.

### Key Motion Principles (Inferred & Verified)

Based on site structure analysis and previous reports:

- **Sequential Reveals (The "Wave"):** Content elements do not appear all at once. They cascade in (staggered delay ~100ms), giving a sense of "flow" rather than static loading.
- **High-Tension Easing:** Motion is not linear. It starts fast and snaps into place with a "premium" feel (using cubic-bezier curves like `0.16, 1, 0.3, 1`).
- **Precision Animation:** Subtle micro-interactions (hover states, pulsing badges) that imply "active intelligence" rather than just decoration.
